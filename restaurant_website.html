Our Story

Val Executive Restaurant was founded in 2023 by Chef Ayebazibwe Vallence and his wife Chef Ainembabazi Mackline. Born in Naples, Italy, Chef Marco trained under Michelin-starred chefs before bringing his passion for authentic Italian cuisine to the heart of the city.

We use only fresh, seasonal ingredients of whichmany imported directly from legal sources to ensure every dish captures the true taste of the Italian countryside.

Our restaurant is open for lunch and dinner, seven days a week. We also offer private dining for special events and group bookings for up to 40 guests.
