Our Story

Since 2005, Val Executive Restaurant has celebrated the culinary traditions of Italy with modern elegance. Founded by Chef Marco Rossi, our kitchen serves handcrafted dishes inspired by regional classics and premium local produce.

  • Founded: 2005
  • Founder: Chef Marco Rossi
  • Cuisine: Authentic Italian
  • Private Dining: Up to 40 guests
